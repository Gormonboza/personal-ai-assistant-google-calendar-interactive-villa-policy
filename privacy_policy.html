Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the application "Personal AI Assistant for Google Calendar — for guests of the interactive villa" ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. Who operates this application

This application is operated by Denis Novikov as part of the Villa Melasti interactive project.

2. What data we collect

When you authorize this app with your Google account, we may access the following data:

  • Your email address (for identification)
  • Your Google Calendar list
  • Your timezone
  • Events from the selected calendars (read access)
  • The ability to create events in selected calendars (write access)

3. Why we collect this data

The data is used to provide you with a personal calendar assistant that can:

  • Display your upcoming events
  • Create new calendar events on your behalf

4. How your data is stored

Your data is stored temporarily in memory on our server while you use the app. No persistent storage or database is used. The app supports only one user at a time.

5. Who has access to your data

Only the app's internal logic (running on the server) and your own interface (desktop app or web) have access to your tokens and calendar data. No third parties are involved.

6. How to revoke access

You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account settings:

https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

7. Contact

For any questions or concerns, please contact Denis Novikov at gormonboza@gmail.com.

Thank you for trusting our assistant.
